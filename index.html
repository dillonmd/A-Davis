About Me!

I am a graduate of Concordia University where I received my MEd in Differentiated Instruction and I hold a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from St. Edwards University. I have been a teacher for over 15 years in the Austin area, for both private and public schools.

I have taught pre-k through 12th grade, in various subject matters. I am passionate about helping struggling students find their own potential to be successful in learning. My philosophy is to adapt the work at hand into a learning model of their comfort with fun activities, encouragement and motivation.

In my spare time, I love spending time with my family and friends. I am currently in the process of returning to school to work on my PhD in education. I love what I do and I find it a dream come true that my passion for the love of learning has become a career.
